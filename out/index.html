Common Signs Your WordPress is Compromised

If you notice any of these, act immediately to prevent data loss and SEO damage.

Random redirects / pop‑ups

Users are redirected to spam or adult sites.

Search warnings

Google marks your site as "Deceptive" or "Harmful".

Defaced pages

Homepage replaced, strange content uploaded.

Unknown admins

New users with admin roles you did not create.

Email blacklisting

Contact forms sending spam or emails bouncing.

Server overload

High CPU usage, slow or down frequently.
